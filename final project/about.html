About us

A Dog's Life specalizes in grooming, boarding, doggy day care, and training in the greater Chicago area. Our family has been servicing the area since 1958.

  • Our groomers have over 200 years combined experience.
  • Our boarding services are clean and spacious
  • We will walk your dog 3 times a day when boarding or day care
  • Need to break your dog of a bad habit? Our trainers have over 30 years experience

Our Mission

Our Mission is to give your pet a stress free grooming experience. We give your fur baby all the tender special care that they could ever need. Woof!
